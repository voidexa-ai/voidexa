## CONCEPT

Planet owners and station workers are the backbone of voidexa's universe. Instead of voidexa building everything alone, the ecosystem grows organically through player-creators who invest time and creativity. voidexa provides the platform, tools, and infrastructure — creators provide the content and earn revenue.

---

## THREE LEVELS OF PARTICIPATION

### Level 1: Station Worker (FREE to start)
- No planet or station required
- Works on other people's planets or at voidexa Hub
- Applies for tasks posted on the Task Board
- Tasks: build ship skins, design race tracks, write missions, create card art, moderate chat, translate content, QA/bug testing
- Revenue: percentage of what they produce (see revenue table)
- Think of it as a freelancer inside the universe
- Rating system: good workers get more tasks and higher pay
- Can upgrade to Station Owner anytime

### Level 2: Station Owner ($100 deposit + $20/month)
- Owns one station (repair, trading, racing arena, workshop)
- Earns from services at their station
- Can hire Station Workers
- Can post tasks on the Task Board
- Can upgrade to Planet Owner later (deposit counts toward planet)
- Revenue share: better than Worker, less than Planet Owner

### Level 3: Planet Owner ($500 deposit + $50/month)
- Owns entire planet with full creative control
- Builds shops, stations, missions, race tracks, PvP arenas
- Hires Station Workers and Station Owners
- Highest revenue share
- Most control and prestige
- Pioneer reward: 10M GHAI (5M+5M, 18mo vesting) — when GHAI launches

## REVENUE SPLITS

### Platform Fee (voidexa's cut)
| Creator Tenure | voidexa Cut |
|---|---|
| Year 1 | 30% |
| Year 2 | 23% |
| Year 3+ | 15% |
| Elite Creator badge | 12% |

Elite Creator: awarded to top 5% creators by revenue or rating. Reviewed quarterly. Badge visible on profile and planet.

30% is industry standard (Steam, App Store, Google Play all take 30%). voidexa's scaling rewards loyalty — no other platform drops to 12%.

### Revenue Distribution Per Transaction
| Transaction Type | Worker | Station Owner | Planet Owner | voidexa | Stripe+Platform |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Worker sells skin on Planet | 40% | — | 15% | 30%* | 15% |
| Station service (repair etc) | — | 40% | 15% | 30%* | 15% |
| Planet shop direct sale | — | — | 55% | 30%* | 15% |
| Worker sells on voidexa Hub | 55% | — | — | 30%* | 15% |
| Race entry fee | — | 35% (arena) | 20% (planet) | 30%* | 15% |
| Card pack (planet exclusive) | 30% (artist) | — | 25% | 30%* | 15% |

*voidexa cut scales down by tenure (30→23→15→12%)
When voidexa cut drops, the difference goes to the creator/owner.

### Minimum Transaction
- No transaction under $0.50 (too little to split meaningfully)
- Stripe minimum applies

## TASK BOARD SYSTEM

### How It Works
1. Planet Owner or Station Owner posts a task: "Need 10 ship skins for my shop — paying 40% of sales"
2. Task appears on the universe Task Board (accessible from any station or the main menu)
3. Station Workers browse and apply
4. Owner reviews applications and selects a worker
5. Worker completes the task and submits for review
6. Owner approves → item goes live in their shop
7. Revenue splits automatically on every sale — forever (worker keeps earning as long as item sells)

### Task Categories
- Ship Skins & Paint Jobs
- Race Track Design
- Mission Scripting (objectives + lore text)
- Card Art Illustrations
- Station Decoration & Theming
- Planet Surface Design (terrain, structures, landmarks)
- Lore Writing & World Building
- Translation (Danish, German, etc.)
- QA & Bug Testing
- Content Moderation
- Marketing & Promotion
- Music & Sound Effects (for planet ambience)
- Tutorial Creation

### Task Board Rules
- Tasks must have clear deliverables and acceptance criteria
- Workers can dispute rejected work (voidexa arbitrates)
- No exploitation: minimum worker share is 30% of revenue their work generates
- Workers own their portfolio (can show work on their profile)
- Non-compete: workers CAN work for multiple planet owners simultaneously
- Quality rating: 1-5 stars, visible on worker profile
- Top-rated workers get "Verified Creator" badge

## WHAT PEOPLE CAN BUILD

### Without a Planet (Station Workers)
- Ship skins (upload textures, preview in 3D)
- Race tracks (place checkpoints, set difficulty)
- Mission scripts (write objectives, lore, dialogue)
- Card art (upload illustrations or generate with AI)
- Bug reports and QA testing
- Chat moderation
- Content translations
- YouTube/streaming content about the universe

### With a Station (Station Owners)
- Everything Workers can do, PLUS:
- Run a repair station (charge for services)
- Run a trading post (marketplace for player-to-player)
- Run a racing arena (host races, charge entry fees)
- Run a ship customization workshop
- Hire Workers for their station

### With a Planet (Planet Owners)
- Everything above, PLUS:
- Full planet surface design (terrain, atmosphere, structures)
- Planet-exclusive shop with unique items
- Multiple stations on their planet
- Host tournaments and events
- Create story missions and lore
- Set planet rules and theme
- Planet-exclusive achievements for visitors
- Revenue from ALL activity on their planet

## CONTENT CREATION TOOLS (provided by voidexa)

### Ship Skin Editor
- Upload textures (BaseColor, Normal, Metallic, Emission)
- Preview on any ship model in 3D
- Color picker for quick recolors
- Template library for common patterns (stripes, camo, gradients)

### Track Designer
- Place checkpoints in 3D space
- Set track difficulty and time limits
- Add obstacles and power-up spawn points
- Test-fly the track before publishing

### Mission Editor
- Write objectives (fly to X, scan Y, collect Z)
- Set rewards (XP, credits, items)
- Add NPC dialogue and lore text
- Chain missions into story arcs
- Set difficulty and time limits

### Card Designer
- Upload card art (512x512)
- Set card stats (energy, attack, defense)
- Write card effect description
- Preview card in-hand UI
- Submit for balance review (voidexa approval required)

### Station Builder
- Choose station type (repair, trading, arena, workshop)
- Customize appearance (color, signage, lighting)
- Set service prices
- Configure operating hours and auto-responses

## QUALITY CONTROL

### Automated Review
- All uploaded content scanned for inappropriate material
- Card balance checked against game economy rules
- Ship skins checked for file size and format compliance
- Race tracks validated for completability

### Community Review
- Players rate planets, stations, and content (1-5 stars)
- Top-rated content promoted in "Featured" section
- Low-rated content flagged for review
- Persistent low quality → warning → temporary suspension

### voidexa Review
- Legendary and Epic items require manual voidexa approval
- Card effects that affect game balance require approval
- Planet themes must comply with Terms of Service
- voidexa retains right to remove any content

## THE ENDGAME

voidexa provides:
- Platform, tools, infrastructure
- Payment processing (Stripe/GHAI)
- Quality control and moderation
- Marketing and discovery
- Game engine and mechanics

Creators provide:
- Content, creativity, engagement
- Customer support for their properties
- Marketing of their own planets/stations
- Community building
- Revenue generation

Jix (you) does:
- Collect 30→23→15→12% of EVERYTHING
- Maintain platform
- Approve high-tier content
- Strategic decisions
- Eventually: very little day-to-day work

This is the Roblox model applied to a space game universe. The difference: voidexa takes far less (12-30% vs Roblox's 75%) and creators keep far more.
