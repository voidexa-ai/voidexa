## WHAT VOIDEXA IS

voidexa is a 3D space playground (not a traditional MMO). Players fly ships through a genuine 3D sphere — 360 degrees in every direction, 1 hour travel time from center to edge. The tone is No Man's Sky (hopeful, curious) + Firefly (warm, human) + Guardians of the Galaxy (humor). There are 6 AI characters (the "Cast") who issue missions and comment on events.

The universe has 5 concentric zones:
- **Core Zone (0-5 min from center):** Safe. Stations, tutorial, shops, Break Room.
- **Inner Ring (5-15 min):** Low risk. Claimed company planets, trade routes.
- **Mid Ring (15-30 min):** Medium risk. Derelicts, nebulae, PvE tier 1-3, exploration signals.
- **Outer Ring (30-45 min):** High risk. Rare resources, pirate hideouts, ghost stations, PvE tier 4-5.
- **Deep Void (45-60 min):** Very high risk. Endgame bosses (The Silent Ones), mythic drops, lore fragments, scanner fog (unidentified dots until approached).

A massive structure called **The Hive** sits in Mid Ring — a Swiss-cheese asteroid with hundreds of tunnels players fly through.

Ship classes: Starter (Bob, free), Fighter, Hauler, Explorer, Salvager.
Currency: Platform-GHAI ($1 = 100 GHAI).
Card game: Turn-based PvE and PvP with 223 mechanic patterns available.
